Welcome to Kryptyk Labs! This guide will help you get started quickly and show you where to find the most important features.

---

## Quick Start

- **Sign up** or **Sign in** from the top-right of the screen.
- Find puzzles on the **Puzzles** page.
- Join or create a team on the **Teams** page.
- View leaderboards to track progress.

---

## Navigation

- **Home** — Overview and quick actions.
- **Puzzles** — Browse puzzles, filter by difficulty and type, and start solving.
- **Teams** — Create or join teams and coordinate with teammates.
- **Leaderboards** — See global and team rankings.
- **Profile / Settings** — Update your account and preferences.

---

## Signing In and Registering

- Click **Sign In** to log in to an existing account.
- Click **Join Now** to create an account.
- If you sign in with OAuth (GitHub/Google), follow the provider prompts.

---

## Starting a Puzzle

1. Visit the **Puzzles** page and pick a puzzle.
2. Click **Start** to begin. Some puzzles are staged and require previous puzzles to be completed.
3. Use hints sparingly — they may affect scoring.

---

## Teams and Collaboration

- Create a team or join an existing team via the **Teams** page.
- Teams can compete on team leaderboards and share progress.

---

## Leaderboards

- **Global** shows individual rankings.
- **Teams** shows team rankings and points.
